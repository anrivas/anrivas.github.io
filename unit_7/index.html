We're a full service riding academy and boarding stable located in beautiful California

We have a wide variety of accommodations and services available. Kool Ranch is located in the Doritoville Foothills, provides excellent care and a beautiful setting for your equestrian needs.

Situated on 100 acres, we are able to provide both a wide variety of boarding accommodations and facilities to ride in many settings. Facility features include: covered arena, all-weather arena, 1/2-mile tree-lined sand track, small covered arena, and a round pen with all-weather sand.

Lessons

We offer lessons of every level with our easy going horses. Kool Ranch offers a tremendous opportunity to learn about horses and improve your confidence in your riding skills. At Kool Ranch, we provide a vast array of horse riding programs to suit a range of abilities and interests. We offer private and group lessons specializing in western riding, dressage, our hunt seat. We also offer Horsemanship Lessons and Liberty Riding Clinics.
